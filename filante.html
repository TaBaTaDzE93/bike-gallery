FILANTE SLR

The natural evolution of Cento10PRO, our high-end aerodynamic bike used by professionals.Filante SLR is an extremely lightweight aerodynamic bicycle with a significantly improved stiffness to weight ratio. We used the same materials and technology as in the construction of the Wilier 0 SLR. The result is a top of the range, aerodynamic frame that complements our pure ascent bike used by Astana Qazaqstan Team.
